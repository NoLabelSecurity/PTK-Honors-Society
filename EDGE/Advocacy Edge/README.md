### **Advocacy Edge**

**BE INTENTIONAL**  
Learn what advocacy really means — define your motivation, understand why advocacy matters, and identify the specific issue you want to address.

**BE INFORMED**  
Research the issue thoroughly from multiple angles, understand key stakeholders and processes, and recognize opposing viewpoints.

**BE COLLABORATIVE**  
Build relationships with collaborators, engage meaningfully with those affected by the issue, and learn how to advocate effectively in a digital world.

**BE THE CHANGE**  
Define measurable goals for your advocacy, create compelling messaging tailored to your audience, and identify the resources needed to take action and evaluate success.
