### **Employment Edge**

**AIM**  
Set your career goals, assess your strengths and skill gaps, explore internship options, and begin building your professional foundation.

**ACT**  
Develop an effective resume & cover letter, build your LinkedIn profile, protect your digital reputation, and learn job-search strategies.

**ADVANCE**  
Enhance networking skills (including elevator pitches), review career fair strategies, and learn the key stages of the interview process.

**ACHIEVE**  
Master interview techniques, craft thank-you letters, understand applicant tracking systems, and gain basics of salary negotiation and benefits packages.
